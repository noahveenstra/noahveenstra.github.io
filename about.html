Growing up in South East Michigan, I've always had a passion for technology. Originating with my interest in entertainment technologies, like video games, I graduated to personal computing devices like the iPod Touch. Continually researching and growing interest for how and why Apple creates products they do, unknowingly, I fell in love with user interface and interaction design.

This newfound love translated well towards one of my other passions, Disney World being one of them, where I had been going since I was a baby. My grandparents were generous enough to prioritize family vacations there, and this began my deep love for Disney and as I grew older, I started to realize why. The guest experience. Disney World has a certain way they operate that allows them to elevate guests and prioritize new and old experiences within their park which is unprecedented.

Currently, I'm in my Junior year at Michigan State University pursuing a B.A. in Experience Architecture. I'm also part of a UX/Web development firm called The Cube @ MSU. We create sites for local businesses and non-profits along with different MSU Offices.
