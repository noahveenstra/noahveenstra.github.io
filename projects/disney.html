how disney world can capitalize on live activity notifications this fall

published june, 2022

published in UX Collective

This year at Apple’s WWDC, they announced a new feature called Live Activity. This feature allows app developers to create a more persistent notification experience. From tracking the score of your favorite sports team to knowing exactly when your Uber will arrive without unlocking your phone. This is a powerful feature for the usability and user experience of certain apps. It allows you to not have to unlock your phone, search for the app, wait for it to load, and finally get the information you want. With Live Activities, you simply just glance at your lock screen and know the status of what you’re waiting on or the event you’re following.
